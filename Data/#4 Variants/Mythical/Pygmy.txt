<span style="color:lightgreen;">Pygmy</span>: Tiny terrors. Pygmys are a miniature version of their species, but don't let that fool
you. Many pygmys can be packed into a very small area, and all Pygmys gain a pack bonus
will all others regardless of Species. Prepare your tiny army!

Pygmy creatures cannot be ridden!
