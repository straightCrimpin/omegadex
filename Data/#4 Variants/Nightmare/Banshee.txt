<span style="color:black;">Banshee</span>: Banshee's release a powerful scream, cursing their enemies and dooming them
to death. The Banshee Scream acts as a powerful debuff, increasing damage taken for all
nearby enemies.

Pressing Prone(X) will cause the Banshee to scream. Nearby enemies are cursed for a short
period and will take increased damage.
