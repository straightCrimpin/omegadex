<span style="color:black;">Zombie</span>: Classic horror creatures in the flesh. Zombies are infected creatures that will turn
their enemies into zombie minions on a killing blow. Those minions are also capable of
turning enemies into zombie minions on killing blow, allowing you to quickly gather up a
horde of infected. This makes the Zombie extremely effective in crowded areas and caves.
Zombies gain bonus movement speed and damage reduction at night.
