Crafting Requirements:
Cosmic Soul: 10.0
Alpha Essence: 50.0
Metal Ingot: 100.0
Crystal: 10.0

Can Only Be Crafted At: Omega Workbench

The Red Dwarf is crafted in the omega workbench. It gives 300% greenhouse effect to all
nearby crop plots, even in caves. It is upgraded with the Blue Star, crafted in the Soul Grinder
for 20 soul shards placed in its trophy slot, and grants 600% greenhouse effect when so
upgraded.
