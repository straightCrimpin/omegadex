Crafting Requirements:
Metal Ingot: 20.0
Polymer: 10.0
Ethereal Soul: 3.0
Basic Essence: 15.0

Can Only Be Crafted At: Omega Workbench

The Dimensional Storage is crafted in the omega workbench. It's a 1000 slot storage container
that also functions as a refrigerator and that can have items set to it so that when they're
dropped into a void vacuum, they will be automatically sent to the assigned storage box.
