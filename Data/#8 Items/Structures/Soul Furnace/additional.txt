Crafting Requirements:
Metal Ingot: 100.0
Stone: 1000.0
Cementing Paste: 100.0
Resource Soul: 30.0
Alpha Essence: 30.0

Can Only Be Crafted At: Omega Workbench

The Soul Furnace is crafted in the omega workbench. It is a structure used to cook meat, craft
jerky and sweet vegetable cakes, and smelt metal. It does all of these things instantly when
equipped with a soul furnace intensifier, crafted for 20 soul shards in the soul grinder
structure.
