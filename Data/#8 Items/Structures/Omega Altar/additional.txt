Crafting Requirements:
Basic soul: 500.0
Delta Essence: 100.0
Stone: 2500.0
Black Pearl: 100.0
Cementing Paste: 500.0

Can Only Be Crafted At: Omega Workbench

The Omega Altar is a tekgram that is unlocked by killing a corrupted master, crafted in the
omega workbench. It is a structure used to summon the later progression bosses with multiple
master and higher souls.
