Crafting Requirements:
Metal Ingot: 20.0
Polymer: 10.0
Utility Soul: 3.0
Basic Essence: 15.0

Can Only Be Crafted At: Omega Workbench

The Quantum Compactor is crafted in the omega workbench. It is a storage container that,
when items without unique data are placed in it and the compact button is pressed, stores
them as data in a list. Items compacted in this way are weightless and cannot spoil.
