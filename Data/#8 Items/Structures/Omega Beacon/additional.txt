Can Only Be Crafted At: Omega Workbench

The Omega Beacon is a tekgram unlocked by killing a prime or higher tier dino, crafted in the
omega workbench. It is a structure used to sacrifice souls to summon dinos to tame (10-99
soul summon) or bosses to kill (100 souls). It is vital to progression.
