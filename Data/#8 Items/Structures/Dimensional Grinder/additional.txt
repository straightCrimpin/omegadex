Crafting Requirements:
Metal Ingot: 20.0
Oil: 10.0
Ethereal Soul: 3.0
Basic Essence: 15.0

Can Only Be Crafted At: Omega Workbench

The Dimensional Grinder is crafted in the omega workbench. It is used to break down both
modded and vanilla items to receive approximately 60% of their crafting materials, and also to
break essence down to lower tier essence at a rate of 1 higher quality essence to 3 lower
quality essence.
