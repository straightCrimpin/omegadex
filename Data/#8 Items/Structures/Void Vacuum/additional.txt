Crafting Requirements:
Metal Ingot: 50.0
Cosmic Soul: 3.0
Alpha Essence: 15.0

Can Only Be Crafted At: Omega Workbench

The Void Vacuum is crafted in the omega workbench. It is a part of the dimensional network
and, when the option is enabled, can send items across the map with no maximum range to
your set mannequins, dimensional storages, dimensional grinder, quantum compactor,
dimensional dumpster, and soul compressor.
